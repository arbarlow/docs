## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    Not directly, but you can cancel and re-subscribe later. Your data remains accessible based on plan limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do prices include tax?">
