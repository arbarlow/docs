## Your Profile

Your Stride profile contains personal information, performance metrics, and preferences that power your training experience.

## Basic Information

### Personal Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile">
    Navigate to **Account → Profile**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Basic Info">
    Update your:
    - Display name
    - Profile photo
    - Bio/description
    - Location (city/country)
    - Birthday (for age categories)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Privacy">
    Control visibility:
    - Public profile
    - Team members only
    - Private (coach only)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Profile** to update
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Profile Photo

**Guidelines**:
- Square format works best
- At least 400x400 pixels
- Under 5MB file size
- JPG or PNG format
- Shows your face clearly

<Info>
  A good profile photo helps teammates recognize you and builds community connection.
</Info>

## Performance Metrics

### Critical Settings

These directly affect training calculations:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="FTP">
    **Functional Threshold Power**
    - Current value in watts
    - Effective date
    - History tracking
    - W/kg display
    
    Update after testing!
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Heart Rate">
    **HR Zones**
    - Max heart rate
    - Threshold HR
    - Resting HR
    - Zone calculation method
    
    Based on recent data
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Weight">
    **Body Metrics**
    - Current weight
    - Target weight (optional)
    - Height
    - Body fat % (optional)
    
    Affects W/kg calculations
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Zone Settings

Choose calculation methods:

**Power Zones**:
- Coggan Classic (7 zones)
- Coggan iLevels (custom)
- British Cycling
- Custom percentages

**Heart Rate Zones**:
- % of threshold
- % of max
- % of reserve
- Custom zones

## Equipment Profiles

### Managing Bikes

Track your equipment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New Bike">
    Click **+ Add Bike**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Bike name
    - Type (road, TT, MTB)
    - Brand and model
    - Weight
    - Components
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set as Default">
    Choose primary bike for activities
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Mileage">
    Monitor component wear:
    - Chain (replace ~3000km)
    - Cassette (6000km)
    - Tires (5000km)
    - Brake pads (varies)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Component Tracking

<Warning>
  Set up component alerts to maintain your bike properly and avoid failures during important rides.
</Warning>

**Tracking Features**:
- Automatic mileage accumulation
- Service reminders
- Cost tracking
- Component history
- Multiple wheelsets

## Privacy Controls

### Activity Privacy

Set defaults for new activities:

| Setting | Visibility | Use Case |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Public | Everyone | Share achievements |
| Followers | Connections only | Semi-private |
| Team | Team members | Team training |
| Private | Only you | Personal data |

### Privacy Zones

Hide start/finish locations:

1. **Add Privacy Zone**
2. **Set center point** (home/work)
3. **Choose radius** (200m-1km)
4. **Name zone** (optional)
5. **Auto-applies** to activities

### Data Sharing

Control what others see:
- ✅ Summary stats
- ✅ Activity titles
- ⚫ Power data
- ⚫ Heart rate
- ⚫ Exact routes
- ⚫ Comments

### Data & Privacy

**Export Your Data**:
- Download all activities
- Export in standard formats
- Backup regularly
- GDPR compliant

**Delete Account**:
- Export data first
- Removes all information
- Cannot be undone
- 30-day grace period

## Public vs Private

### Public Profile Benefits

- Appear in searches
- Share achievements
- Inspire others
- Build following
- Coaching opportunities

### Staying Private

Valid reasons:
- Professional concerns
- Personal privacy
- Competitive advantage
- Minimal social needs

You can still:
- Use all features
- Join private teams
- Work with coaches
- Track progress

## Tips for Great Profiles

### Do's ✅
- Use real photo
- Update FTP regularly
- Accurate weight
- Meaningful bio
- Current location

### Don'ts ❌
- Fake information
- Inappropriate photos
- Inflated FTP
- Spam in bio
- Privacy oversharing
