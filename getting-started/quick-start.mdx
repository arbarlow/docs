## Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Stride! Follow these steps to get started with your training journey.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [stride.is](https://stride.is) and sign up with your email.
    
    <Tip>
      Stride uses password login. You'll get a one time login link to your email.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Devices">
    Stride works best when connected to your training devices:
    
    - **Garmin** - Automatic activity sync, metrics, and workouts.
    - **Wahoo** - Activity and workout sync
    - **Whoop** - HRV data
    - **Strava** - Activity names and routes
    
    [Learn more about device connections →](/getting-started/connecting-devices)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Let Stride Analyze Your Training">
    Stride will automatically analyze your training data and get to work calcalating metrics like Training Score, Load, and Fatigue.

		Stride can estimate your **FTP (Functional Threshold Power)**, **Heart Rate Threshold**, and **Running Threshold** for you. You can also set your own custom zones.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the Mobile App">
    Get the Stride app for recording activities and following workouts:
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="iOS App Store" icon="apple">
        Download for iPhone and iPad
      </Card>
      <Card title="Google Play" icon="android">
        Download for Android devices
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
    
    [Mobile app setup guide →](/getting-started/mobile-app-setup)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Training">
    You're ready to go! Here's what you can do next:
    
    - **Browse Training Plans** - Find a plan that matches your goals
    - **Analyze Your Data** - View your Training Score, Load, and Fatigue
    - **Join a Team** - Train with others or add your coach
    - **Plan an activity** - Add a future activity to your calendar, and get weather information
  </Step>
</Steps>
