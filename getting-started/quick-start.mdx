## Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Stride! Follow these steps to get started with your training journey.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [stride.is](https://stride.is) and sign up with your email or continue with Google.
    
    <Tip>
      Use the same email address associated with your Garmin or Wahoo account for easier device syncing later.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Devices">
    Stride works best when connected to your training devices:
    
    - **Garmin Connect** - Automatic activity sync
    - **Wahoo SYSTM** - Activity and workout sync
    - **Whoop** - Recovery and HRV data
    - **Strava** - Activity names and routes (not the actual activity data)
    
    [Learn more about device connections →](/getting-started/connecting-devices)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Training Zones">
    For accurate training recommendations, set up your:
    
    - **FTP (Functional Threshold Power)** - Your cycling baseline power
    - **Heart Rate Zones** - For effort-based training
    - **Weight** - For power-to-weight calculations
    
    [Configure your zones →](/getting-started/setting-zones)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the Mobile App">
    Get the Stride app for recording activities and following workouts:
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="iOS App Store" icon="apple">
        Download for iPhone and iPad
      </Card>
      <Card title="Google Play" icon="android">
        Download for Android devices
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
    
    [Mobile app setup guide →](/getting-started/mobile-app-setup)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Training">
    You're ready to go! Here's what you can do next:
    
    - **Browse Training Plans** - Find a plan that matches your goals
    - **Create a Workout** - Build custom training sessions
    - **Record an Activity** - Use the mobile app or sync from your device
    - **Analyze Your Data** - View your Training Score, Load, and Fatigue
  </Step>
</Steps>
